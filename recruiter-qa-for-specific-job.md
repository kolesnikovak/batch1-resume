## Problem-Solving Questions

### Q17: "Can you give an example of a challenging testing problem you solved?"

**Answer:**
"At PepsiCo, our test suite had 12% flakiness, causing false failures and slowing releases. I analyzed failure patterns and identified timing issues and environment inconsistencies. I reduced flakiness from 12% to under 2%, which restored team confidence and accelerated our release cycle."

❌ Don't claim you can do everything—be honest about gaps
